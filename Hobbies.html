My Hobbies

Hello again.
I don't know why would you want to know more about me, but Welcome anyway!


My hobbies are reading, listening to music, daydreaming, and talking to myself.

I started reading novels in 5th grade. It was a novel series by Laura Inggals Wilder. After that, I just started to read every novels I can get my hands into.
My favourite authors are Cassandra Clare and Rick Riordan. Sometimes when I read, I kind of forget that I'm reading. It feels like I'm actually inside the book. I really really love reading.

I always love music. I never really have any fixed genres. If the song flows, it flows. My favourite songs/singers/bands are all from different genre.
